Payroll Processing Flow

Follow this sequence to avoid payroll mismatch or missing attendance records.

1. Employee Master Verify employee salary, department, role, UAN, ESIC and bank details. 2. Attendance Upload Upload present days, weekly off, paid leave, holiday, LOP and overtime. 3. Reports & Payslips Download payroll register, employee payslips and HR audit report.

Paid Days Present + Weekly Off + Paid Leave + Holiday - LOP.

Salary Earnings Basic, DA, HRA, special allowance, other allowance and gross salary.
03

04
